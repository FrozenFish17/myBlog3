School Church

Was established in 1906

Pierrepont School Church was a chruch founded with the school. It has gotten upgrades as time as passed by but nothing to big. Excpet they're planing on shutting down the chruch for a few years as they add new classrooms, a bigger team, and sanitary upgrades.



School Library

Was built 1917

The School Library was founded after the school. It was made original as a safe place with the library a differnt smaller part that was made with the school. After World War 1 ended the school deciced to change the library to the new location were they now use for the school book fair and the classroom for the library class.



School Auditorium

Was built 1912

The School Auditorium was founded after the school. It was made after many complants from parents, teachers, and students who didn't like meetings in a cramped area so they made this.
